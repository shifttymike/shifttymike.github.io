This is my blog. Yes, another one… (waits for groaning to die down and eye-rolling to stop)

My name is Michael and I’m an electronics engineer, ex sound engineer, new ham, doing computery things and aspiring to a role in Information Security.

The purpose of this blog is:

  • To keep a record of things I’ve done and how I’ve done them, both for sharing what I learn with others and as a reference for future, dumber me
  • To have a “finish line” for projects. Explaining something to others is a great way to both confirm that you understand, as well as cement your own knowledge
  • To serve as some kind of a portfolio
  • To learn about static sites and the process of getting it from Markdown to live on a browser
  • To get rid of Wordpress. Tsek!
